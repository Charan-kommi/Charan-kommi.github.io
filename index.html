About Me

Who Am I

I'm Sai Charan Chowdary Kommi, a cybersecurity enthusiast focused on offensive security, red teaming, and vulnerability research. I thrive on understanding how systems can be compromised — so they can be better defended.

My journey began with a curiosity for how software works under the hood, which quickly evolved into exploring exploitation techniques, reverse engineering, and network intrusion. I actively participate in CTF competitions and bug bounty programs to sharpen my skills in real-world scenarios.

When I'm not hacking machines on HackTheBox or TryHackMe, I build open-source tools to automate recon, analysis, and exploitation workflows — and write about what I learn.
